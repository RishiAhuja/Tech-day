Technology Day

The Indian government declared 11 May as National Technology Day to commemorate the Pokhran nuclear tests that were carried out on 11 May 1998.

Why we celebrate technology day?

National Technology Day 2021 on May 11 celebrates the achievements and contributions of Indians to science and technology. This day serves as a reminder of India breaking into the elite group of nations with nuclear weapons. The National Technology Day was observed for the first time on May 11, 1999.
